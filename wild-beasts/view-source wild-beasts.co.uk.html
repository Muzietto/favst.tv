<h2 class="news-title">Soft Future / Blood Knowledge</h2>
<span class="news-body">
<p>Today Wild Beasts release two previously unheard tracks entitled Soft Future and Blood Knowledge below. Soft Future is the band’s first ever instrumental track, and is available to download for free as a special end-of-year giveaway, <a style="text-decoration:underline;" href="http://po.st/SFDownload" target="_blank">HERE</a>. You can buy both tracks at <a style="text-decoration:underline;" href="http://po.st/SoftFutureiTunes" target="_blank">iTunes</a>.</p><p>Over the past couple of months Wild Beasts and Parisian animator Mattis Dovier have been working together to create an <a style="text-decoration:underline;" href="http://po.st/SFNovel" target="_blank">interactive GIF-Novel</a> to accompany Soft Future and Blood Knowledge. The project forms part of The Jameson Works which is all about supporting modern-day craftsmen and sharing the interesting stories behind their processes. You can <a style="text-decoration:underline;" href="http://bit.ly/creatingthegifnovel" target="_blank">watch</a> the band creating the GIF-Novel and read an article about the project in <a style="text-decoration:underline;" href="http://po.st/GIFCreativeReview" target="_blank">Creative Review</a>.</p><iframe width="100%" height="250px" scrolling="no" frameborder="no" src="https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?url=https%3A//api.soundcloud.com/playlists/63729796%3Fsecret_token%3Ds-mQ1en&amp;auto_play=false&amp;hide_related=false&amp;show_comments=true&amp;show_user=true&amp;show_reposts=false&amp;visual=true"></iframe><br><br />
<p>Present Tense – Special Edition, featuring the original album augmented by a bonus disc of remix material from artists including Factory Floor, Juan Atkins, Lone, East India Youth, Sohn as well as a brand new <a style="text-decoration:underline;" href="http://po.st/PalaceFoalsSC">remix of ‘Palace’ from Foals</a>, is available to purchase now.</p><p>Buy Present Tense – Special Edition at <a style="text-decoration:underline;" href="http://po.st/PresentTenseSEiTunes" target="_blank">iTunes</a> or <a style="text-decoration:underline;" href="http://po.st/PresentTenseSE" target="_blank">Dom Mart</a>.</p> </span>
